Noche de Industria

SHPE USF Career Fair Landing Experience

A project to better prepare students for SHPE career fair

As the External Director for the Society of Hispanic Professional Engineers (SHPE), I led a project to improve the "Noche de Industria" career fair experience. Recognizing that many students were unprepared and lacked information about participating companies, my team and I developed a modern and user-friendly website to address these issues and ensure attendees were well-informed and ready to engage. In this project we were able to:

  • Create an intuitive and visually appealing website using React and Tailwind CSS, providing easy access to event details, company profiles, and job opportunities
  • Enabled hundreds of students to come better prepared to the career fair, leading to more productive and meaningful interactions with potential employers
  • Gathered and analyzed demographic and engagement data from over 400 SHPE members using JavaScript and Python (NumPy, Pandas, Matplotlib), providing companies with valuable insights to build stronger partnerships with the organization

SpeakFix

English Practice with AI

I built an AI chatbot to help my relatives speak English more fluently.

Learning a new language can be challenging, especially without a practice partner. People often resort to boring textbooks or expensive apps and tutors. Moreover, effective learning requires frequent practice at any time that fits one's busy work schedule, which real tutors can't always provide.
That's why I created SpeakFix, an AI-powered English chatbot that offers convenient, interactive, and personalized practice for my relatives. Ten of them have used it and seen significant improvements.

  • The integration of Groq AI API, Llama 3, and Google Neural Voice enabled the chatbot to engage in natural and dynamic conversations with users
  • Using advanced prompting techniques, I customized the chatbot to tailor the learning experience for each user's needs and conversation style. This resulted in a 30% average increase in conversational English fluency within just two months for 10 of my relatives
  • The user interface, built using the Vue.js framework, was praised by non-technical users for its ease of use
